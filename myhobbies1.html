About Me

Hi! My name is Annie Mzembe and I’m studying Computer Science at the University of Limerick. I’ve always been curious about how technology works and how we can use it to build creative, useful things. When I’m not working on assignments or learning something new, I like to unwind with a good story, a great show, or a fun game.


Things I enjoy the most:


  • Drawing and illustrating anime-inspired art
  • Reading romantasy books, manga, and comics
  • Watching TV shows — especially Game of Thrones, House of the Dragon, and Star Trek
  • Watching anime (my comfort genre!)
  • Playing games like The Sims, Minecraft, and mobile MOBAs
  • Exploring creative projects that mix art and technology
  • Learning about how computers and logic actually work (even if it drives me crazy sometimes )
